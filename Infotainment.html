Infotainment System



Infotainment in EV

ROZIK software addresses the users their present milage through which at every interval of 10000 kms the users would receive rewards. It also will show the users the real-time traffic and the fastest route to reach their destination with an integrated gps in our software.

  1. GPS navigation with real-time traffic updates, and the ability to search for points of interest and plan routes.
  2. AM/FM radio, satellite radio, and the ability to connect to music streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, and more.
  3. Integrated with the car's climate control system, allowing the driver to adjust the temperature and airflow inside the car.
  4. Telematics system, which allows the driver to access information about the vehicle's performance, such as battery status, charge level, and range.
